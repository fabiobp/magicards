Privacy Policy for Magi Cards

Last Update: October 1, 2025

"Your privacy is not in game"

At Bibiu Games ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to creating original and memorable gaming experiences through Magi Cards. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our game, websites, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Services.

1. What is Personal Data?

"Personal Data" (or "Data") is any information that identifies you, either:

  • Directly: such as your name, email address, or phone number
  • Indirectly: such as your account ID, IP address, device identifiers, cookie IDs, and gameplay data

2. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not provide any information through our Services.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe we might have information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at contact@bibiugames.com.

Users between 13 and 17 should have parental or guardian consent before using our Services.

3. What Data We Collect and Why

a) Account Creation

Username, email address, password (encrypted), date of birth, country/region. Your username is public so other players can find you.

b) Third-Party Sign-In

When you sign in via Google, Facebook, Apple, Game Center, or Google Play Games, we collect the information you authorize those services to share (name, email, user ID, profile picture).

c) Purchases

Transaction history, purchase amounts and dates, payment method type. Payment details are handled securely by payment processors - we do not store complete credit card information.

d) Gameplay

Levels completed, cards collected, achievements, scores, rankings, time played, in-game actions, progress, and any other game-related data necessary to provide and improve our Services.

e) Device and Technical Data

Device type, operating system, device IDs, IP address, browser type, connection timestamps, crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics.

f) Communications

When you use in-game chat or contact support, we collect your messages, username, and timestamps to maintain a safe community and provide assistance.

g) Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, understand usage patterns, improve performance, and deliver relevant advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide Services: Account management, gameplay features, purchase processing, customer support
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage, fix bugs, develop features, balance gameplay
  • Communicate: Service updates, newsletters, promotional offers (with consent)
  • Safety & Security: Prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse; enforce our Terms
  • Personalize: Customize experience, recommend content, display relevant ads
  • Comply: Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: To provide Services you requested
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve Services and ensure security
  • Consent: For marketing and personalized ads (you can withdraw anytime)
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

6. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Unity Cloud, AWS), payment processors, analytics, customer support platforms
  • Advertising Partners: For relevant ads (opt-out via device settings)
  • Other Players: Your username, profile, statistics, and achievements are visible
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country, including the United States. We use standard contractual clauses and other safeguards approved by regulatory authorities.

8. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to provide Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Account data is retained while active and for a reasonable period afterward. You can request deletion by deleting your account.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data via contact@bibiugames.com
  • Correct: Update account information in settings or via contact@bibiugames.com
  • Delete: Request account deletion via contact@bibiugames.com (some data may be retained for legal reasons)
  • Object/Restrict: Object to certain processing or request restriction via contact@bibiugames.com
  • Withdraw Consent: For marketing emails or personalized ads anytime via account settings or contact@bibiugames.com
  • Opt-Out of Ads: iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads

10. Data Security

We implement encryption, regular security assessments, access controls, and staff training to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Services may link to third-party websites or social media. We're not responsible for their privacy practices - please review their policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We'll notify you by posting updates, changing the Last Update date, and sending email for significant changes. Continued use means acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests: contact@bibiugames.com (Subject: "Privacy Inquiry - Magi Cards")

You have the right to file a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.
